What is Trakila ?

Trakila is a new unique online shopping research destination, which helps track prices of items on popular Indian online shopping websites. It is a friendly and useful software you would like by your side before deciding when and where to buy your favourite item from. Trakila helps to track prices of items online,and notifies the users whenever the price of the item goes down.

Just provide us the url of the item whose price you want to track. You can tweet us the url of the item, and leave the rest to us. Trakila fetches the present price of your item and notifies you abou the same. You can then forget about it, sit back and relax, until Trakila notifies you again when the price of your item goes down. We advice you when and from where to buy the item, for the cheapest price. You can also check out the real time price graphs to understand the trend of the price of the item
